# Design Principles

## Product tone
- calm
- clinical
- clear
- trustworthy
- not flashy

## UX principles
- prioritize actionability over decoration
- show why something matters
- reduce cognitive load
- support dense clinical information without clutter
- make states obvious: loading, empty, warning, error, success

## Visual principles
- readable hierarchy
- strong spacing
- theme consistency
- clear status semantics
- charts optimized for legibility

## Accessibility principles
- keyboard support
- focus visibility
- sufficient contrast
- non-color cues where practical
